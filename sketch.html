Courses we offer

The introductory course is the starting point for anyone who is just beginning to learn the art of DJ-ing.
It's suitable for most people and expertly designed to teach you all the fundamental skills and theory yo will need.
All gears/equipments necessary for this course would be provided
though it would be of great advantage for students to have personal equipments for the sake of personal reviewing and practice.

Beginner

  • Access to Multi-Genre Music Library
  • Downloading and Organizing Music Collection
  • Proper Utilization Of Dj Mixing
  • Basic Music Theory

Intermediate

  • Advanced Music Theory
  • Synchronization/Beat Matching
  • Track Structure and Timing For Mixes
  • Seamless Mixing

Master

  • How To Use Fx
  • How To Prepare/Record Your Set
  • Basic and Advanced Scratching
  • How To Use Industry Standard Deck
